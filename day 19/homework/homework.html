My Favourite Foods

Pizza Pepperoni

Pepperoni pizza is cheesy, crispy, and topped with spicy pepperoni slices. It's my favorite comfort food!

French Fries

French fries are golden and crunchy potato strips, perfect with ketchup or cheese sauce.

Fried Eggplant

Fried eggplant is soft inside, crispy outside, and full of flavor. I love eating it warm with garlic sauce!
