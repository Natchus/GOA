Banana Oat Pancakes

Mash banana in a bowl, then add eggs and oats. Mix well. Then cook it.

Tomato and Cucumber salad

chop tomato and cucumber in a bowl. Add pinch of salt and olive oil. Mix well.

Yoghurt with honey and nuts

put yoghurt in a bowl. add honey on top. Then sprinkle some nut and enjoy.

garlic butter pasta

Boil pasta in salted water. In a pan melt butter and fry garlic until golden. Add pasta and little bit of pepper.
