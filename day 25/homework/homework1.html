Creamy Garlic Butter Pasta

ingridients:

  • Pasta (any kind)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • ½ cup heavy cream or milk
  • salt, pepper
  • parmesan

Cook pasta according to package.
In a pan, melt butter and sauté garlic for 1–2 minutes.
Add cream or milk, season with salt and pepper.
Add cooked pasta, stir to coat. Top with cheese if you have it.

Egg Fried Rice

ingridients:

  • 1 cup cooked rice (cold rice works best)
  • 2 eggs
  • soy sause
  • green onion
  • oil

Heat oil in a pan, scramble the eggs, then set aside.
Add more oil, toss in rice, break it up and fry for a few minutes.
Add soy sauce, eggs, and any veggies. Mix well and serve.

One-Pan Baked Chicken & Veggies

ingridients:

  • chicken thighs or breast
  • Chopped veggies (carrots, potatoes, bell peppers)
  • olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika

Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
Toss chicken and veggies with oil and spices on a baking tray.
Bake for 30–35 minutes until chicken is cooked through and veggies are tender.

Avocado Toast with Egg

ingridients:

  • Bread (toasted)
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1 egg (fried, poached, or scrambled)
  • Salt, pepper, chili flakes or lemon juice (optional)

Mash avocado with a pinch of salt and lemon juice.
Spread on toast, top with your cooked egg.
Sprinkle chili flakes or pepper for extra flavor.

Greek Yogurt Fruit Parfait

ingridients:

  • Greek yogurt (plain or vanilla)
  • Fresh or frozen berries
  • Granola
  • Honey (optional)

In a glass or bowl, layer yogurt, berries, and granola.
Drizzle honey on top if desired.
Serve immediately.
